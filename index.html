When should I use the dryer vs the steam straightener?
Hair Dryer if your primary need is drying wet hair faster, adding volume, or enhancing curls with the diffuser. Steam Straightener if your hair is already dry and you want it smoother, straighter, and frizz-free — especially if you have thick, curly, or coarse hair. Many buyers own both: dryer after washing, straightener for styling days.
