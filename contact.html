Frequently Asked Before Contacting

Q: Can you review my product?
We're always open to reviewing new hair tools. Email us with product details, and our team will evaluate it for potential inclusion.

Q: I found an error in a review. How can I report it?
We appreciate corrections! Please use the contact form and select "Correction / Update" as the subject, and include the specific page URL and the error details.

Q: Do you offer advertising or sponsored content?
We do not accept sponsored reviews. However, we may consider relevant advertising partnerships. Select "Partnership / Advertising" and tell us about your proposal.

Q: I have a question about a product you reviewed.
For product-specific questions (warranty, usage, specifications), we recommend contacting the manufacturer directly. For questions about our review, feel free to reach out.
