Wavytalk Hair Dryer Guide

Everything you need to know about the Wavytalk Ionic Hair Dryer: who it is best for, how to use the attachments, and whether it fits your hair type.

Who is this dryer for?

The Wavytalk Blown Away is a 1875W ionic ceramic hair dryer with 3 attachments (concentrator, diffuser, comb) at $33. It is the #1 Best Seller in Hair Dryers on Amazon with 24,197 verified reviews and a 4.4-star rating.

It is designed for home users who want faster drying with less frizz at a fraction of the cost of premium brands like Dyson or Shark. Many 4C-hair buyers specifically praise the comb attachment for stretching coils while drying.

Best hair types

  • 4C / thick / coily hair — the comb attachment stretches and dries in ~10 minutes
  • Curly / wavy hair (2A-3C) — the diffuser enhances definition without frizz
  • Normal / medium hair — fast drying on medium heat, smooth ionic finish
  • Fine hair — works on low heat + cool shot, but some report hair going flat

Attachment guide

Comb Attachment

The fan favorite. Detangles while drying. 4C and thick hair buyers report finishing their entire head in 10 minutes. Stretches coils without needing a separate brush.

Diffuser

For curly and wavy hair. Distributes airflow gently to set curls in their natural pattern. Use on LOW heat, hold still for 20-30 seconds per section.

Concentrator Nozzle

For precision blowouts. Directs airflow to smooth specific sections. Best for straight-to-wavy hair or finishing touches after rough-drying.

Common complaints

  • Button placement — controls sit where fingers naturally grip, causing accidental presses
  • Durability — some report motor getting loud after a few months of heavy daily use
  • Fine hair goes flat — use low heat + cool shot to maintain volume
  • 125V only — does not work internationally without a voltage converter
  • Cord length — some buyers find it too short for their bathroom setup
